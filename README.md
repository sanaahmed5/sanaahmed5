<h2>🌸 &nbsp;Hey, I'm Sana! &nbsp;<img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="28"/></h2>

<p width="60%">

I'm a passionate and ambitious developer who believes that great design and powerful code are not opposites — they're the perfect partnership. 💜

I started my journey falling in love with the visual side of the web — crafting pixel-perfect interfaces with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**. Today, I build full-featured sites with **React**, architect **WordPress** experiences that clients adore, and I'm actively pushing deeper into the backend world with **Node.js and Express.js** — on my way to becoming a confident **Full-Stack Developer**.

But I don't stop at code. I'm equally obsessed with **growth** — how traffic flows, how audiences are built, and how the right digital strategy turns a quiet website into a thriving platform. I specialize in **Website Traffic Generation**, **Traffic Arbitrage**, and **Digital Marketing**, blending technical skills with strategic thinking to create web experiences that don't just look good — they *perform*.

</p>
